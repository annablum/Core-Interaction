Reinterpret what's on your mind and find meaning in the abstarcted narrative.
This project is meant to 'activate' the shape poem by Bruce Usher and Antti Kalevi. Inspired by their collaborative project I have created an experience of typing your own shape narrative. Bruce Usher and Antti Kalevi created a unique alphabet code through shapes and symbols, a shape translater allows you to see your thoughts and ideas through shapes.
